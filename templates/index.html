Welcome to NeedSeva!


Due to the COVID19 pandemic, there have been many changes in our daily routines. One of the important and everyday tasks involves grocery and life-essential goods shopping, which has transitioned to online operation. This transition might not seem like a major problem for the young generation but for the older generation, it could be a bit of hassle. These are difficult times, we all have to stand together and help each other during this uncertain situation. So we created this platform to provide a bridge to connect the elderly population with the community volunteers that are willing to devote their time in tackling this global pandemic.


Here's how it works:


On the next screen, you'll fill out a registration form.

Then, you'll add a task you need to be completed: an errand to run, a pet to walk. You name it.

A friendly volunteer will contact you to complete the task.
